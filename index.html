Enter the Slipgate

Slipgate Ironworks is a game development studio located in Aalborg, Denmark.

We’re comprised of industry veterans and talent, having worked on both indie and AAA titles for the past 2 decades.

Our first game, released in 2013, was the critically acclaimed „Rise of the Triad”, and was considered by many as the birth of the modern retro shooter.

Since then, we’ve been involved in a range of games, and used everything from the newest technology to the original „QUAKE” engine, in order to realise our shared creative vision of „only working on games we’d play ourselves”

We all come from a background of hard-core first person shooters and we all share a deep knowledge of how games of the past have influenced generations.

Some might even call us old-school, but that's a badge we wear proudly.

Welcome, and enter the Slipgate Ironworks.
